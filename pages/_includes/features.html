Private by Design

Secure Networking

🚦 Datura Firewall gives you fine-grained control over network access for all of your apps.

🤝 Tethered network devices (USB or Wi-Fi) can use the phone's VPN or Tor.

🥸 Free and trusted VPNs from Tor and Riseup.

🌎 Cloudflare DNS is available as a Private DNS provider.

Limitless Functionality

✌️ [[microg]] available as a Google Play Services alternative to use core services, such as notifications, while maintaining much more anonymity and privacy.

🗺️ Positon or BeaconDB (and locally acquired AP data) available as privacy-friendly location services.

📍 Nominatim available as the default geocoding service.

Device Security

📆 Automatic monthly over-the-air (OTA) security updates.

⏲ Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, camera, and microphone can be automatically turned off when not in use.

🔐 Verified Boot ensures that the operating system has not been modified.

🛑 Restrict USB helps you block unknown USB devices.

Private Dialer

📞 Dialer comes with built-in support for Signal and WhatsApp calling options.

🤫 Sensitive Numbers hides helpline numbers, such as domestic violence, child abuse, or suicide hotlines from your call log.

Secure Backup with Seedvault

⏰ Automatic backups (once configured) of your apps.

☁️ Option to back up your files to a USB drive or Nextcloud.

🔐 Client-side encryption protects all of your backup data.

Work-life balance

💼 Maintain work-life balance with Work Profile. Use one device for both work and personal purposes.

💡 Apps and data in Work Profile are isolated from your main profile.

Your Favorite Apps

🎁 Get free and open-source apps from F-Droid.

🛍 Use Aurora Store to install from the Google Play catalog anonymously.

Browse without Censorship

🦆 DuckDuckGo is our default search engine.

🥸 Use Tor Browser to browse the internet without being tracked.
